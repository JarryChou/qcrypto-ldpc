29  usage: getrate [-i unfile] [-t time] [-o oufile] [-t time] [-n events]
30  [-s ] [-6 | 8] [-c]
31 
32  options/parameters:
33 
34  -i infile: source of the raw data stream from the timestamp card.
35  If not specified, stdin is chosem
36 
37  -o outfile: target for the count result. if not chosen, stdout is used.
38 
39  -t time: integration time in 1/8 nanoseconds. If nothing is specified,
40  1 epoch length or 2^29 nsec is chosen.
41 
42  -n evts: terminates after evts rounds of counting. if evts=0, it loops
43  forever. default is evts = 1.
44 
45  -s : split detector results. If this option is set, four colums
46  corresponding to individual detector events are given, plus a
47  fifth one for the overall events (can be lower than sum of 4
48  due to coincidences). Switched off by default.
49  -6 : same as -s option, but with 6 detectors, where the two
50  additional ones are identified by 1-2 and 2-3 coincidences
51  -8 : same as -6 option, but with two more detectors corresponding
52  to coincidences 3-4 and 4-1
53  -c : compensation option. If this is set, the single counts get also
54  incremented if there is a coincidence event, e.g., a
55  coincidence event between detectors 1 and 2 contributes also to
56  singles 1 and 2 if this option is set; otherwise, it does not
57  contribute to the singles.
